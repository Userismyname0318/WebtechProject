| Additional Information |

So, you're thinking about joining the International State College of the Philippines? Great choice! Here's how to navigate our admissions process with ease and a smile:

1. The Application Adventure: First things first, you’ll need to complete our online application form. It’s user-friendly and fun—think of it as the first quest in your educational adventure. Be honest, be yourself, and don’t forget to include your favorite meme (just kidding, but we do love a good sense of humor).

2. Document Scavenger Hunt: Gather your transcripts, letters of recommendation, and that award you won for best science fair project in 10th grade. Submit them along with a personal essay. We love to hear about your dreams, aspirations, and, of course, your quirkiest hobby.

3. The Entrance Exam Extravaganza: Next up is our entrance exam. But fear not—it’s designed to showcase your strengths, not trick you with brain teasers. We want to see your potential, your creativity, and how you think outside the box. Plus, there’s a rumor that the exam includes a bonus question about which superhero would make the best computer programmer (we're team Iron Man, by the way).

4. Interview with a Twist: If you make it past the entrance exam, you'll be invited for an interview. But this isn’t your typical, nerve-wracking Q&A. Think of it as a friendly chat with one of our faculty members or even our beloved mascot, the Cosmic Carabao. We want to know what makes you tick, what makes you passionate, and what your favorite pizza topping is (pineapple is acceptable, but controversial).

5. Acceptance Celebration: Congratulations, you’ve been accepted! Cue the confetti cannon and virtual high-fives. You'll receive your official acceptance letter, along with a welcome package that includes your very own ISCP mug and a beginner’s guide to surviving our legendary campus traditions.

6. Enrollment Extravaganza: Finally, complete your enrollment by choosing your courses, paying your fees, and attending our fun-filled orientation week. Meet your fellow adventurers, explore the campus, and get ready to embark on the most exciting journey of your life.

Ready to start? Head to our admissions down below and let the adventure begin. At ISCP, we don’t just admit students; we welcome new members to our family. So, pack your curiosity, bring your enthusiasm, and prepare to make memories that will last a lifetime. See you on campus!

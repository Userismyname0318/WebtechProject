Mission

"To boldly go where no educational institution has gone before, providing an education that's out of this world. At ISCP, we aim to mold students into global citizens who are proficient in everything from astrophysics to underwater basket weaving. Our mission is to equip our students with the knowledge, skills, and superpowers needed to conquer their dreams, all while enjoying the occasional free pizza party."

Vision

"To be the most eccentric and unforgettable institution in the galaxy, where learning is an adventure, and no question is too bizarre. We envision a future where our graduates not only excel in their chosen fields but also master the art of balancing a textbook on their head while riding a unicycle. ISCP strives to be a beacon of innovation, creativity, and general wackiness, producing alumni who are as unique as they are brilliant."

Some Of Our Offered Courses


Bachelor Of Science In Information Technology


Do you find yourself mystified by the digital realm, entangled in a web of zeros and ones? Welcome to the world of Information Technology (IT), where we demystify the enigma of cyberspace and transform you into a maestro of modern marvels. In our IT course, you'll journey from tech novice to digital virtuoso, mastering everything from hardware hocus-pocus to software sorcery. Imagine your computer as a magical box; we'll teach you to unlock its secrets. You'll learn to converse with machines in their own language, wielding coding spells to make them dance to your command. From crafting elegant websites that dazzle the eyes to building robust networks that withstand the wrath of digital gremlins, you'll become a tech wizard capable of feats unimaginable. But it's not just about tinkering with gadgets. Our IT course dives deep into the digital ocean, exploring data analytics, cybersecurity, and cloud computing. Picture yourself as a cyber sleuth, protecting the virtual kingdom from hackers and rogue AIs. Or as a data whisperer, uncovering hidden truths and trends that could save the world (or at least boost a company’s bottom line). We'll also delve into the ethics of IT, ensuring you wield your newfound powers responsibly. Because with great power comes great responsibility—especially when you're the one preventing a server meltdown at 3 AM. So, if you’re ready to swap your confusion for confidence and join the ranks of the tech-savvy elite, our Information Technology course is your gateway. Get ready to code, connect, and conquer the digital frontier. Your journey to becoming a digital demigod starts here.




Bachelor Of Science In Computer Science


Ever wondered what makes your smartphone smart or how video games render those epic dragon battles? Enter the world of Computer Science (CS), where we decode the mysteries of computation and turn you into a digital alchemist. In our CS course, you'll transition from curious newbie to master coder, unlocking the secrets of algorithms and data structures along the way. Think of your computer as a puzzle box. We'll show you how to solve it using the power of logic and creativity. You'll learn to write programs that perform complex tasks, whether it's developing an app that could become the next social media sensation or creating software to analyze massive datasets in the blink of an eye. Our journey doesn't stop at coding. You'll explore the architecture of computers, understanding what goes on under the hood. Ever been curious about how your computer's brain—its CPU—works? We'll demystify it, teaching you how to optimize performance and troubleshoot issues like a pro. Beyond the nuts and bolts, our CS course dives into artificial intelligence, machine learning, and robotics. Imagine crafting an intelligent assistant that predicts your needs or designing robots that can navigate a maze. You'll be at the forefront of innovation, pushing the boundaries of what's possible. Ethics in CS is also a critical component. With great coding power comes great responsibility. You'll learn about privacy, security, and the moral implications of technology, ensuring that your creations benefit society while respecting individual rights.




Bachelor Of Science In Computer Enginering


Have you ever pondered what lies behind the sleek exterior of your gadgets or dreamed of designing the next revolutionary device? Welcome to the realm of Computer Engineering (CE), where we blend hardware and software magic to shape the technology of tomorrow. In our CE course, you'll evolve from a tech enthusiast to a master engineer, capable of crafting the next generation of computing marvels. Think of your devices as intricate puzzles; we'll equip you with the skills to piece them together. You'll dive into the world of circuits and microprocessors, learning how to design and optimize hardware that forms the backbone of our digital world. From crafting efficient processors to creating innovative sensors, you’ll become a wizard in the art of hardware engineering. But it’s not all soldering irons and circuit boards. Our CE course bridges the gap between hardware and software, teaching you to write the code that breathes life into machines. You'll learn to develop firmware that makes devices smart and responsive, creating everything from embedded systems in smart appliances to the control algorithms for autonomous vehicles. Our curriculum also delves into emerging technologies such as the Internet of Things (IoT), robotics, and advanced computing systems. Imagine engineering a network of smart devices that communicate seamlessly or designing robots that perform complex tasks autonomously. You’ll be at the forefront of technological innovation, turning science fiction into reality.
